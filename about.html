How the B&B Was Established

 

- Founded by Daniel Lin, a 48-year-old designer and lifelong nature enthusiast, Scandinavian Cabin Retreat reflects his belief that calm, simplicity, and warmth can transform a stay into a meaningful experience. After years of traveling through Nordic regions and studying minimalist design, Daniel wanted to create a quiet sanctuary where guests could slow down and reconnect with nature.

- What began as a personal project, renovating a small lakeside cabin, slowly grew into a full B&B. Daniel spent years refining every detail, from the architecture to the interior textures, inspired by Scandinavian comfort and Asian craftsmanship. Scandinavian Cabin Retreat opened its doors with a single goal: to offer guests a “peaceful, thoughtful, and beautifully designed place to rest.
